<h3 id="1">[1] Hardware-Software Co-Design for Event-Driven SNN Deployment on Low-Cost Neuromorphic FPGAs</h3>
- **arXiv**: [2604.22179](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22179)
- **Authors**: Jiwoon Lee, Souvik Chakraborty, Syed Bahauddin Alam, Cheolsoo Park
- **Subjects**: cs.AR
- **Tags**: Hardware-Software Co-design, Neuromorphic Computing, FPGA
- **Summary**: 本文提出了一种软硬件协同设计框架，可将PyTorch定义的脉冲神经网络(SNN)确定性地部署到事件驱动的FPGA上执行。该框架在MNIST分类任务上实现了87.40%的准确率，并达到了0.1375μs/图像的服务延迟和31.6nJ/图像的动态能耗。
